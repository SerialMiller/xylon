# Xylon Park Tuddies 🏈

Browser-based backyard football. Pure slot machine energy.

Inspired by the rugged, unforgiving competition that took place at **Xylon Park, St. Louis Park, MN** — where legends were made, reputations were destroyed, and no one agreed on the rules.

## How to Play

**[Play it here →](https://serialmiller.github.io/xylon/)**

Draft your squad of 3. Call a play. Pray.

You get 4 downs to punch it in. Most people don't.

## The Secret

There's a combination of player and play that changes everything. You'll know it when you see it.

## Built with

One HTML file. No frameworks. No mercy.
